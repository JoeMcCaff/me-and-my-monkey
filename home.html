Who Are We?

Donegal On Track is a campaign group for reconnecting County Donegal to the Irish rail network.

The primary focus of this group is to pressure the governments of Dublin and Belfast to commit to building our proposed lines as soon as possible. This group asserts that a fast, frequent and efficient rail network is neccessary for supporting the growing population of Ireland, for creating businesses, and tourism. Therefore, Donegal should be part of the rail network.

The secondary focus of this group is to advocate for better public bus transport in County Donegal.
